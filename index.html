The Jorge Luis Borges Hotel

The Jorge Luis Borges Hotel is located at the frontier between Argentina and the Dominican Republic. Each room at the JLB Hotel is named after a short story by the literary master. In said room there are televisions where the story which the room is named after, is read 24 hours, 7 days a week.

The price for each room is measured in how many letters its namesake story contains.

Go to the Dominican Republic and drive South.
